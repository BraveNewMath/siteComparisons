Site Notes
YESSERA Does not have a tag line.  Although it uses the expanded version of its acronym as the tag line:  YESSERA means Young Ethioipians Self-Sufficiency Enhancing Re-education Association.  We feel that this is a belabored way to deliver a tag line.  We believe the tag line is an opportunity to make the elevator pitch, and YESSERA is letting that opportunity pass by.
Wegene Wegene's tag line is "Rebuilding families one child at a time".  It is prominently displayed on the site.
PPL Project for Pride in Living's tag line is "Helping people help themselves."  It is also Which captures the goal of the organization more fully than the previous two.
